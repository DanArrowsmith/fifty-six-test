Open Water Diver

Perfect for - beginners wanting to learn all the basics and become fully fledged open water divers in a fun environment. Maybe you've done a DSD and are eager for more, or you just know that you're keen to get straight on with the certification! Our schedule for our Open Water courses is normally spread out over 3 ½ days.

Orientation start day at 4pm. You get to meet your instructor and fellow class mates (note - we only ever allow 4 divers per instructor, so you get plenty of one to one time.) You will complete some paperwork and watch the first training DVD to give you a feel for what you'll be up to. This will normally only take 2 hours, and we can then answer any questions you might have.

Day 1 - The course usually starts at 4pm where you will meet your instructor and we ease you in to the course with a few videos to get you excited about what’s to come. Day 2 - In the morning, we will continue with theory to ensure that by your 2nd afternoon of diving, you’ll be confident in learning some new skills. Day 3 - Lets Go Diving! We will head out on the FIFTY56SIX boat and put all those skills to good use! Final Day - It’s an earlyish start, but it just means when you get back there is plenty of time to celebrate that you are now OPEN WATER CERTIFIED!

We will always adapt the pace of the class to meet individual needs. Sometimes people need more time to get comfortable. Please also note that the schedule may be subject to change due to unavoidable environmental and/or weather factors. Loved your open water course, but want to take it even further?
